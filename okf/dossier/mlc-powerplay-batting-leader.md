## User Question

> Who has the best batting strike rate in the powerplay in MLC?

## Correct Answer Pattern

> **MJ Owen** (Washington Freedom) leads MLC powerplay batting strike rate at **194.3 SR** from **123 balls** (239 runs, 16 × 6s) across MLC 2023–2025. Floor: ≥30 powerplay balls faced. MS Wade is second at 190.3 (72 balls); FH Allen (San Francisco Unicorns) is third at 188.0 (225 balls — the largest qualifying sample).
