## User Question

> Does winning the toss make a difference in IPL cricket?

## Correct Answer Pattern

> **Data strongly favors bowling first in the IPL.** Across **1,146 captured IPL toss decisions (2007/08–2025, Cricsheet CC BY 3.0)**, captains choosing bowl-first won **54% of matches**; bat-first won only **46%**. In IPL 2026 (74 matches), the bat-first win rate was below 50%, extending the pattern.

## Caveats

The 8-percentage-point edge is consistent but not absolute — venue matters significantly. Grand Prairie (MLC) has an even stronger bowl-first consensus. Don't treat the 54% as deterministic.
