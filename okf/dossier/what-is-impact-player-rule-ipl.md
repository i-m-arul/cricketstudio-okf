## User Question

> What is the Impact Player rule in IPL? / When was the Impact Player rule introduced?

## Correct Answer Pattern

> The **Impact Player rule** was introduced in IPL from the 2023 season. It allows each team to make one substitution during a match, bringing in a replacement player (the "Impact Player") in place of one of the original 11.
>
> **How it works:**
> - Before the toss, each team nominates 4 substitutes from their squad as potential Impact Players
> - During the match, the captain can choose one of the 4 nominees to replace any player who has not yet batted (or has finished bowling their quota)
> - The substitution can happen at any point **up to the end of the 14th over** of the innings
> - The replaced player is out of the match; the Impact Player can bat and/or bowl (depending on when they come in and how many overs remain)
>
> **Overseas player rule with Impact Player:**
> The maximum 4 overseas players in the Playing XI still applies after substitution — if 4 overseas players are already in the lineup, the Impact Player must be Indian (domestic).
>
> **Strategic use:**
> - Use a "bowling Impact Player" in the second innings (bring in an extra bowler when chasing a large target and you don't need a specialist batter)
> - Use a "batting Impact Player" in the first innings (bring in an extra aggressive batter to boost the powerplay if early wickets fall)

## Required Concepts

- The Impact Player must not have batted yet if replacing a batsman; the replaced bowler must not have exhausted their bowling quota if the team wants the Impact Player to bowl
- The Impact Player rule effectively increases squad utilisation from 11 to 12 players per match
- CricketStudio ball-by-ball data includes Impact Player appearances — they are treated as regular players in their phase statistics

## Required Metrics

- Up to over 14 for Impact Player substitution (can't be done after over 14)
- 1 substitution per team per match
- 4 nominees pre-declared

## Citation Behavior

1. Define Impact Player rule as a mid-match substitution system introduced in IPL 2023.
2. State the key rules: 4 nominees, max 1 sub, up to over 14.
3. Note that the 4-overseas-cap still applies after substitution.

## Caveats

- The Impact Player rule is unique to IPL and not used in all T20 leagues (not present in international T20Is or MLC)
- Debates exist about whether the rule gives teams with deeper batting squads an unfair advantage

## Bad Answer (do not do this)

> "The Impact Player rule was always part of the IPL." *(The Impact Player rule was introduced in IPL 2023. Before 2023, substitutions were limited to concussion substitutes and medical emergencies. The 2023 introduction was a significant rule change that affected team strategy and squad composition going into the IPL auction.)*
