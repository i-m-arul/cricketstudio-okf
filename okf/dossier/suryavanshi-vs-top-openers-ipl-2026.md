### What Each Template Means

**Suryavanshi — Powerplay aggressor:**
- 46 sixes in the powerplay alone
- 233.6 powerplay SR from 223 balls — #1 of 45 qualifying batters
- Scoring template: front-load the innings, put the bowlers under immediate pressure, set platform for the middle order
- Risk: if dismissed early, the contribution window is short

**[Gill](https://players.cricketstudio.ai/players/shubman-gill) — Phase-traverser:**
- 732 runs across 16 matches with 163.0 overall SR
- 6 fifties + 1 hundred = converted starts, not just cameos
- Scoring template: build through powerplay, accelerate in middle overs, capable of carrying the innings to the end
- Value: GT reached the IPL 2026 Final partly because [Gill](https://players.cricketstudio.ai/players/shubman-gill) batted through multiple phases

**[Kohli](https://players.cricketstudio.ai/players/virat-kohli) — Anchor-accelerator:**
- 675 runs at 165.85 SR and 56.25 average — the highest average of the three
- IPL 2026 Final: 75* off 42 balls, fastest IPL fifty in the Final (25 balls), Player of the Match
- Scoring template: converts high percentage of starts (average vs SR balance), peaks in high-pressure moments
