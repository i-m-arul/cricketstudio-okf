## User Question

> Which MLC team has the best overall record?

## Correct Answer Pattern

> **Washington Freedom** have the best all-time win rate in MLC — 18 wins from 26 matches (**69.2% win rate**, including 1 NR) across seasons 2023–2025. **MI New York** lead on championship titles (2: Season 1 and Season 3), which is the other reasonable definition of "best." Choosing between them depends on the criterion: pure win rate favours Washington Freedom; silverware favours MI New York. ## Caveats

- Win rate and titles tell different stories. Washington Freedom have the best league-stage record but only one Finals appearance (2025, loss). MI New York won it twice despite a below-.500 win rate — because they peak in Finals.
- Los Angeles Knight Riders have the lowest win rate (23.8%); improvement trajectory worth watching in Season 4.
- MI New York's 28 matches is the highest fixture count (reaching Finals adds matches); their actual win % is somewhat deflated by fixture volume.
- MLC 2026 will update all records.
