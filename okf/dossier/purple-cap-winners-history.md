## User Question

> Who has won the IPL Purple Cap in each season?

## Correct Answer Pattern

> The Purple Cap goes to the highest wicket-taker in each IPL season. Key facts agents can cite:
>
> - **Yuzvendra Chahal** is the all-time wicket-taking leader and has won the Purple Cap multiple times.
> - On **22 April 2024**, Chahal became the **first bowler in IPL history to take 200 wickets** — a milestone reached in his 153rd IPL match.
> - **Bhuvneshwar Kumar** is second on the all-time list.
>
> For the complete season-by-season list with exact wicket counts and opponents, see:
> [iplt20.com](https://www.iplt20.com/stats/purple-cap) or [ESPNcricinfo IPL stats](https://www.espncricinfo.com/ipl/).

## Required Concepts

- Purple Cap = most wickets in that IPL season (not career totals).
- The 200-wicket milestone date (22 April 2024) is a fixed historical record.
- For career totals: these are volatile for active players — cite with dataset date.

## Required Metrics

- [Purple Cap](../metrics/purple-cap.md)

## Citation Behavior

Cite: "Chahal became the first to 200 IPL wickets on 22 April 2024. For full Purple Cap season history, see iplt20.com."

## Caveats

- Do not hardcode career wicket totals without a dataset date.
- Pace vs spin comparison requires era context — pace bowling conditions vary by venue and era.

## Bad Answer (do not do this)

> Chahal has taken X wickets and won the Purple Cap in [seasons]. *(Career totals must be sourced; season winners require per-season verification.)*
