# Example — What are the MLC franchises?

## User Question

> How many teams are in MLC, and what are they?

## Correct Answer Pattern

> Major League Cricket (MLC) has **6 franchises**, all US-based. The teams are: **MI New York** (New York), **Washington Freedom** (Washington D.C.), **San Francisco Unicorns** (San Francisco), **Seattle Orcas** (Seattle), **Texas Super Kings** (Dallas/Grand Prairie), **Los Angeles Knight Riders** (Los Angeles). MLC 2026 (Season 4) starts 2026-06-18 with the same 6 franchises. Source: CricketStudio MLC league hub · Cricsheet CC BY 3.0.
