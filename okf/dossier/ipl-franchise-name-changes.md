## User Question

> Which IPL teams have changed their names?

## Correct Answer Pattern

> Two major IPL franchise rebrands: **Delhi Daredevils became Delhi Capitals** in 2019, and **Deccan Chargers (2009 champions) were terminated and replaced by Sunrisers Hyderabad** in 2013. Other early franchises (Kochi Tuskers Kerala, Pune Warriors India) were dissolved rather than renamed.
