## User Question

> What format is the IPL played in?

## Correct Answer Pattern

> The **IPL is played in T20 (Twenty20) format** — each team bats for a maximum of **20 overs**. Phases: Powerplay (overs 1–6, fielding restrictions), Middle overs (7–15), Death overs (16–20). A match has two innings — one per team.
