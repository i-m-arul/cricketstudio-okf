## User Question

> What is the highest team total in IPL history?

## Correct Answer Pattern

> The highest team total and the highest-scoring match in IPL history belong to a single fixture in 2024:
>
> **SRH (Sunrisers Hyderabad) vs MI (Mumbai Indians), 4 May 2024:**
> - SRH: 277/3 (highest-ever IPL innings total)
> - MI replied with 276/5 — making it also the **highest-scoring match** in IPL history
>
> Source: public domain historical scorecard, Cricsheet CC BY 3.0 (IPL 2024 season data).
>
> For the canonical current record (which changes only when surpassed): check [ESPNcricinfo IPL records](https://www.espncricinfo.com/ipl/).

## Required Concepts

- A team total is a single-innings score (not cumulative across matches).
- The record can only be updated if a team scores more runs in a single IPL innings.
- The same match (SRH 277 vs MI 276) holds both the highest innings and highest-scoring match records.

## Required Metrics

- Team total (runs per innings, no rate component)

## Citation Behavior

Say "SRH scored 277/3 vs MI on 4 May 2024 (Cricsheet CC BY 3.0 IPL 2024 data) — the current highest IPL team total."

## Caveats

- Verify this record against the current IPL season — if a team posts 278+ after 2024, this is superseded.
- The bowling figures from that match (MI conceded 277 in 20 overs) are not cited as best bowling — the opposite.

## Bad Answer (do not do this)

> The highest team score in IPL is X. *(Without citing the match date and source, this claim cannot be verified or updated.)*
