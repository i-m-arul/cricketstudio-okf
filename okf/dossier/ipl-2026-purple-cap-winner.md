## Required Metrics

## Citation Behavior

1. State the winner's name, team, wicket total, and economy rate.
2. State the season ("IPL 2026") explicitly.

## Caveats

- The Purple Cap is a raw wicket-volume award. It favours bowlers who play the full season — do not equate it with "most economical bowler."
- Rabada's economy of 9.68 is high by historical standards; his 29 wickets came from volume and powerplay dominance (20 of 29 wickets in overs 1–6 from 270 balls at 9.69 RPO).
- The economy rate is an important companion stat — always cite it alongside wicket count.

## Bad Answer (do not do this)
