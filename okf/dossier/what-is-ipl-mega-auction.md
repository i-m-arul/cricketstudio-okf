## User Question

> What is the IPL Mega Auction?

## Correct Answer Pattern

> The **IPL Mega Auction** is a full squad reset auction held every 2–3 IPL seasons. Unlike the Mini Auction (annual, incremental), the Mega Auction puts virtually every player back into the pool:
>
> | Feature | Mega Auction | Mini Auction |
> |---------|-------------|-------------|
> | **Frequency** | Every 2–3 years | Annual (off years) |
> | **Squad reset** | Near-complete — franchises release most players | Partial — franchises fill gaps |
> | **Retentions** | Up to 6 players retained (varies by BCCI rules) | Existing squad largely kept |
> | **Purse** | Full ₹120 crore cap minus retention costs | Remaining purse from prior season |
>
> **Process:** Before the auction, each franchise submits a retention list (up to 6 players). All other contracted players re-enter the pool. On auction day, franchises bid in real-time for each player in the pool. A player's base price is a minimum bid; franchises raise bids until one remains.
>
> Most recent Mega Auction: **IPL 2025** (held November 2024). IPL 2026 used a Mini Auction to fill squad gaps.

## Required Concepts

- "Right to Match" (RTM) cards historically let franchises match a rival's winning bid to retain a player — rules vary by cycle
- Overseas player slots: each team may field 4 overseas (non-Indian) players per match; auction includes both Indian and overseas players
- The Mega Auction creates the most squad volatility — star players change franchises regularly (e.g., Virat Kohli from RCB for his entire career is an exception)
- "Marquee players" go to auction in early sets with higher base prices

## Citation Behavior

1. Define Mega Auction as a full squad reset every 2–3 years.
2. Contrast with Mini Auction (annual, partial).
3. Note most recent Mega Auction was IPL 2025 (November 2024); IPL 2026 used Mini Auction.

## Caveats

- Exact retention rules (number of retentions, RTM availability) vary by cycle and are decided by BCCI.
- CricketStudio does not track auction bid amounts; refer to IPL/BCCI official releases.

## Bad Answer (do not do this)

> "The IPL Mega Auction happens every year." *(The IPL Mega Auction is held every 2–3 years — a full squad reset. Annual IPL events use a Mini Auction where franchises fill squad gaps rather than rebuilding from scratch. The most recent Mega Auction was before IPL 2025.)*
