## User Question

> How many players have appeared in MLC? How has the player pool grown?

## Correct Answer Pattern

> MLC's player pool grew from **83 players** in Season 1 (2023) to **110 players** in Season 3 (2025), with **166 unique players** having appeared across all three seasons. Season 4 (2026) has 115 registered players pre-season — the largest pre-season roster in MLC history. Source: CricketStudio aggregation of Cricsheet CC BY 3.0 · snapshot 2026-06-20.

## Caveats

- "Players used" counts players with at least one match appearance per Cricsheet records. Not all registered players appear in matches.
- The 166 unique figure means many players appeared in only one or two seasons — career-average comparisons should specify which seasons.
- Season 4 (2026) in-season appearances will differ from the 115 pre-season registered figure.
