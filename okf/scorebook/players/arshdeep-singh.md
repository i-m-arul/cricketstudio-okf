# Arshdeep Singh

## Summary

Arshdeep Singh is a left-arm fast bowler for Punjab Kings in the IPL and India across white-ball formats (T20I and ODI). A specialist new-ball and death-overs bowler, Arshdeep is regarded as one of India's most important T20 bowling assets, known for his ability to swing the ball with the new ball and execute yorkers in the death overs.
