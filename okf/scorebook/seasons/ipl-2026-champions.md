# IPL 2026 Champions — Royal Challengers Bengaluru

## Summary

Royal Challengers Bengaluru won **back-to-back IPL titles in 2025 and 2026**, defeating
Gujarat Titans by 5 wickets in the 2026 final at the Narendra Modi Stadium on 2026-05-31.
They are only the **third franchise in IPL history** to win back-to-back titles.
